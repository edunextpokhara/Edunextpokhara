Welcome to शिक्षा साथी Home Tuition Network! Please follow the systematic workflow below to complete your application, verification, demo classes, and ongoing teaching responsibilities.

Application Workflow & Guidelines (आवेदन र कार्यप्रणाली)

1
१. भ्याकेन्सी छनोट (Vacancy Selection)
First, carefully review and select the vacancy that best suits your preference and location.
2
२. आवेदन तथा परिचय प्रमाणीकरण (Application & Identity Verification)
Confirm the location and submit your application. Afterwards, verify your identity by uploading your face photo and ID card/Citizenship via the provided link.
3
३. शुल्क, ठेगाना र डेमो क्लास (Fee Payment & Demo Class)
Parent’s contact details will be provided only after paying the specified percentage fee. Following this, a 2-3 day demo class must be conducted.
4
४. फिर्ता नीति र आचारसंहिता (Refund Policy & Code of Conduct)
If rejected during the demo class, the fee will be refunded. However, fees are non-refundable once the demo is approved or if terms are breached (e.g., contacting parents directly outside our channel).
5
५. परीक्षा र नतिजा पोर्टल (Exams & Result Portal)
Tutors must conduct weekly exams, prepare marksheets, upload them to our Result Portal, and submit copies to both parents and शिक्षा साथी.
6
६. भुक्तानी प्रक्रिया (Payment Process)
Tutors must collect their monthly tuition payment directly from the parents.
⚠️ विशेष सूचना (Important Notice)
संस्थाको अनुमति बिना व्यक्तिगत रूपमा अभिभावकसँग पढाउने सम्झौता गरेमा वा अनुशासनहीन काम गरेमा ट्युटरलाई तुरुन्तै हटाइनेछ।
Direct agreement with parents without organization consent or violation of rules will lead to immediate termination from the platform.
